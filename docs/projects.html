Emperor Edelgard Von Hresvelg

Edelgard von Hresvelg is a major character from the Nintendo franchise "Fire Emblem"'s newest title 'Three Houses' released in 2019.
In Part I of the game, she is introduced as the heir apparent to the throne of the Adrestian Empire.
In Part II, she has been crowned Emperor and wields a Crest Weapon specially designed for her, a bone axe named Aymr.

This project is a life scale replica of Aymr, with electronics implemented for visual FX.
However, depending on the final worktime constraint, there may or may not be extra content to be updated. Stay tuned.
